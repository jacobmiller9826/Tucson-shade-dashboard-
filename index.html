Building Shade for Tucson

Tucson is one of the hottest cities in the U.S. This project maps shade coverage and proposes new shade infrastructure — solar canopies, trees, and covered walkways — to help residents cool down.

Why Shade Matters

  • 🌳 Reduces heat islands and saves energy
  • 😌 Makes walking and biking safer and comfortable
  • 🌞 Supports community gathering spaces
