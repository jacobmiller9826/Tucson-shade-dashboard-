About the Tucson Shade Network

We’re building a cooler Tucson — one shaded street, park, and plaza at a time.

☀️ Our Mission

Tucson’s heat is intensifying. Asphalt, concrete, and sparse greenery make it one of the hottest metro areas in the country. Our mission is to create a community-driven shade infrastructure — blending data, design, and local action to keep Tucson livable.

🌳 What We Do

  • Map existing shade (trees, canopies, covered walkways)
  • Identify heat islands and unshaded transit corridors
  • Propose and visualize new shade projects
  • Connect community members, nonprofits, and city planners

📍 Project Inspiration

Just as Minneapolis built skyways and tunnels for the cold, Tucson can design a network of connected shaded spaces for the heat. We’re reimagining urban comfort for a desert future.
